I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Computer Science at the Florida State University. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Xin Liu. My research focuses on wireless sensing and security, developing contact-free biometric systems for health monitoring and authentication. By combining signal processing and machine learning, I enable ubiqutious Wi-Fi devices to sense human vital signs and identities without cameras or wearables, extending wireless sensing from single-user to multi-user scenarios through spatial signal modeling.
